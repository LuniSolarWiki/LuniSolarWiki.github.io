Economy & Shop

This guide covers all the ways to make money, sell items, and trade with other players on Lunisolar.

On this page

  • Server Selling (/sell)
  • ChestShops (Player Shops)
  • Auction House (/ah)

Server Selling (/sell)

This is the most direct way to make money. As you mine your OneBlock, you will get a lot of basic materials. You can sell these directly to the server for a fixed price.

Simply use /sell in-game (many players use /sell all) to sell all sellable items from your inventory.

ChestShops (Player Shops)

ChestShops are the best way to create your own shop on your island and sell items to other players, even when you are offline. You can set these up at your /pwarp for visitors.

How to Create a Shop

Follow these exact steps to create a shop:

  1. Place a Chest on your island.
  2. Hold the item you want to sell in your hand.
  3. Left-click the chest with the item.
  4. Type the price you want to sell one of that item for in chat.
  5. Stock the chest with the items you are selling. Your shop is now open!

How to Edit a Shop

If you need to change the price or other settings, simply right-click the chest to open the shop editor.

Auction House (/ah)

The Auction House (AH) is the server-wide, temporary marketplace. This is the best place to buy and sell rare items, tools, crate keys, and other high-value goods.

How to Buy

Type /ah to open the Auction House. You can browse all items for sale or search for something specific.

How to Sell

To sell an item, hold it in your hand and type /ah sell. A menu will appear asking you to set the price and quantity. Be careful, as items listed on the AH usually have a listing fee and expire after a certain time.
