Starting Your Island

To get started, you will want to do /ob create and then you will be sent to your island. If it doesn't send you, then do /ob go.

üí∞ Making Money

Next, you're going to want to know how to make money. First, you can mine your oneblock and /sell the items you get from there. You can also ask other higher players for money, but it won't always work out.

üîë Crate Keys

You can get all crate keys for free by voting and playing for the server! Do /vote and vote for the server with the 5 different links (or visit our Vote Page). Once you are done, you should get 5 vote keys. You can go to /warp crates and use your vote keys.

You'll be able to get:

  • Common, Epic, and Legendary
  • Common x2, Epic x2, and Legendary x2

The only key you can't get from votes is KoTH keys.

üó∫Ô∏è Player Warps (pwarp)

With these player warps, you can set a public arena which players can warp to. You can set up shops and many more by doing this.

  • Create a warp: /pwarp create {name}
  • Go to a warp: /pwarp {name}
  • Manage a warp: /pwarp manage {name}
  • Delete a warp: /pwarp remove {name}

üè™ ChestShops / PlayerShops

ChestShops allow you to sell items while offline and have a shop. To make one, you need to left-click a chest with the item you want to sell and type in chat the amount for it to go for. Then, put the item of choice in the chest.

If you need to edit the shop later on, you need to right-click it.

‚öñÔ∏è Auction House (AH)

To use the auction house to buy items, do /ah and pick the item you want to buy. If you want to sell an item, do /ah sell.

üëë KoTH (King of the Hill)

In /warp pvp, there will be a KoTH every 3 hours. You have to go to the middle and sit there for 3 mins. Once you have sat there without leaving it, you will get a KoTH key which gives you the chance to get OP armor and a seasonal LootBox.

üéÅ Envoys

Envoys happen every hour. You can get money, keys, armor, and tools. There are 3 tiers of envoys:

  • Common: Gives common keys and money.
  • Rare: Gives epic keys and money.
  • Legendary: Gives money, armor, and tools.

üéâ Party Games

To play fun games while bored, do /pg join and you can select 1 of 3 games to play: BlockParty, Spleef, and Survival Games.

‚öîÔ∏è Duels

To duel someone, you can duel with your armor or a selected kit. Do /duel {player} to duel someone.
