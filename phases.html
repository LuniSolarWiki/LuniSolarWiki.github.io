Oneblock Phases

As you break your OneBlock, you will progress through different phases. Each phase unlocks new blocks, items, and mobs. Here is the list of phases in order of progression.

Phase Progression

After the final phase, the cycle will repeat, starting back at the beginning.

  1. Plains
  2. Underground
  3. Winter
  4. Ocean
  5. Jungle
  6. Swamp
  7. Dungeon
  8. Desert
  9. Plenty
  10. Desolation
  11. Deep Dark
  12. Lush Caves
  13. Dripstone Caves
  14. Mangrove Swamp
  15. Meadow
  16. Cherry Grove
  17. Jagged Peaks
