Ranks & Perks

Here are all the buyable ranks available on Lunisolar. Each rank includes all the perks from the ranks below it.

All ranks are available for purchase on our Server Store.

Buyable Ranks

These ranks are a one-time purchase and give you permanent perks.

Nebula

  • [Nebula Kit]
  • 2 Player Vaults
  • /anvil - Opens a virtual anvil.
  • /sit - Sit down anywhere.
  • /lay - Lay down anywhere.

Eclipse

Includes all perks from Nebula, plus:
  • [Eclipse Kit]
  • Upgraded to 3 Player Vaults
  • /ec - Opens your Ender Chest.
  • /seen {player} - See when a player was last online.
  • /bellyflop - Perform a bellyflop.

Solar

Includes all perks from Eclipse, plus:
  • [Solar Kit]
  • Upgraded to 4 Player Vaults
  • /fly - Enable flying on your island.
  • /craft - Opens a virtual crafting table.
  • /condense - Condenses items into blocks.
  • /nick - Set a custom nickname.

Lunar

Includes all perks from Solar, plus:
  • [Lunar Kit]
  • Upgraded to 5 Player Vaults
  • /feed - Fills your hunger bar.
  • /disposal - Opens a virtual trash can.
  • /rename - Rename the item in your hand.
  • /skull {player} - Get a player's head.
  • /keyall - Give everyone online a vote key.

Lunisolar

The ultimate donor rank! Includes all perks from Lunar, plus:
  • /repair - Repair the item in your hand.
  • 3x Seasonal Lootboxes
  • Custom tag in chat.

Seasonal / Monthly Ranks

These special ranks are found in Seasonal/Monthly Lootboxes. They are mainly for show and offer access to powerful perks.

  • Includes permissions from Lunar rank and below.
  • Comes as a "withdrawable" item, so you can trade or keep it.
  • Grants a unique, temporary prefix to show off.
